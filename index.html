Short Information on Processed Food Products

What are Processed Foods?

Processed foods are items that have been altered from their natural state for safety, convenience, or flavor. Most foods we eat are processed in some way, such as pasteurization of milk to remove harmful bacteria.

Examples include bread, breakfast cereals, cheese, cakes, biscuits, and tinned fruits and vegetables.

Common Examples with

  • Packaged snacks
  • Packaged bread
  • Cereal
  • Processed meat
  • Condiments
  • Sweetened and alcoholic beverages
  • Candy and desserts

Source: MD Anderson Cancer Center.

Healthier Processed Options

Some processed foods can be good for you, such as fresh and frozen fruits and vegetables, plain yogurt, dried beans, roasted nuts, eggs, meats, and milk.
